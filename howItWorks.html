How it works

Essential Elements of a Successful Recycling Program

coordinator

A capable and enthusiastic program coordinator for an effective education and awareness program with a simple and reliable collection system.

bins

Weighs 6 lbs. Made of 100% recycled plastic. Capacity 6 cu. ft. (44 gallons) 21" diameter and 30" deep inside (not including space under the lid); 35" tall with lid.

recyclables

A secure market for the PET recyclables. (How much will you have). A qualified, reliable, full-service outside reclamation company (a recycler or waste hauler) .

location

Each school will determine an outside collection location. Collection containers will be provided by URRC.

transportation

A representative from Spartanburg Coca-Cola will collect all recyclables from each school on a regular basis and “back haul” the material and take to URRC.
